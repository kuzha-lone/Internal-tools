# Flux 2 Pro Prompting Guide

## Key Principles

1. **Be descriptive and specific** — Flux responds well to detailed natural language prompts
2. **Specify the style** — "hyper realistic", "raw", "cinematic", "studio photo", etc.
3. **Include technical details** — camera angle, lighting, lens type
4. **Describe the subject clearly** — age, hair color, expression, pose, clothing
5. **Set the scene** — background, environment, time of day, atmosphere

## Portrait Image Prompts (1080x1920)

Since we generate at 1080x1920 (9:16 portrait), compose prompts for vertical framing:
- Subject should fill the frame vertically
- For people: head-to-waist or full body works best
- Avoid wide landscape compositions

## UGC Avatar Prompts

Template:
> "super realistic photo of a beautiful [age] year old [hair color] hair-colored [race] girl that's super [emotion/reaction]; at a [setting], hyper realistic, raw, focus on realism and crystal clear images of the people"

Vary these elements each time:
- **Age**: 22, 23, 24, 25
- **Hair**: blonde, brunette, black, red, auburn, platinum
- **Race**: diverse — vary across generations
- **Emotion**: shocked, excited, amazed, laughing, surprised, intrigued
- **Setting**: bedroom with laptop, coffee shop, cozy apartment, office desk, kitchen counter, living room couch

## Product Prompts

For product showcase images:
- Focus on the product in an aspirational setting
- Use dramatic lighting — studio lighting, ring light, golden hour
- Show the product in use or in an elegant display
- Include lifestyle context that matches the target audience

## Benefit/CTA Prompts

For the final CTA clip:
- Show the result/outcome of using the product
- Happy, satisfied person in an improved situation
- Warm, inviting lighting
- Aspirational but relatable setting
